Multi-Stimuli-Responsive Smart Materials for Soft Robotics

A detailed overview.

Objective

Hydrogels are porous hydrophilic polymer networks that exhibit properties of both solids and liquids. They are multi-stimuli-responsive, largely bio-compatible, and broadly applicable in the field of soft robotics.
To addresses the lack of modelling dedicated for any specific of the material, I built a multi-physics simulation model, coupled with experimental validation, for the actuation response of a NaAc/AAm hydrogel under an electric field.
